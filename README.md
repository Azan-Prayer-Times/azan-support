## Need Help?

If you're experiencing an issue, please include the following information when contacting support:

* App version
* Device model
* iOS version
* A description of the issue
* Steps to reproduce the problem (if possible)
* Screenshots (optional but helpful)

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Prayer times look incorrect

Please verify:

* Your location permission is enabled.
* The selected calculation method matches your region.
* Your device date, time, and time zone are correct.

### Notifications are not working

Please check:

* Notifications are enabled in **Settings > Notifications > Azan**.
* Focus Mode or Do Not Disturb is not preventing notifications.
* Prayer notifications are enabled within the app.
